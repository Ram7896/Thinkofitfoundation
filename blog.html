WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN THE YOUTH LEADERSHIP PROGRAM? What’s in it for me? A question that must be lingering upon your mind, fret not because we value your precious time and thought process more than you can imagine. Before you are a leader, success is all about Growing Yourself. When you become a leader, success is all about Growing Others. Jack Welch in Winning, 2005 These truly profound words by Jack Welch summarize what you gain by investing your time and letting us nurture your efforts into meaningful attempts at changing the world. The Youth Leadership Program is about equipping you with the skill set required to be a leader while ensuring your growth. You will get a chance to collaborate with the young leaders of our nation. We believe that you are capable of being mature and understanding and that leadership doesn’t necessarily have much to do with age. COURSE OF PROGRAM
